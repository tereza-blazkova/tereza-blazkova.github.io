Why I Started a Personal Website

I created this website to have a personal space where I can share my research, thoughts, and academic journey. My primary aim is to provide insights into my research on algorithm auditing and responsible AI in education. I hope to make complex topics accessible to a broader audience and keep you updated on my ongoing projects. Additionally, I want this site to serve as a platform for collaboration, inviting others to engage with my work or propose new ideas.

What You Can Expect

Over time, I plan to publish blog posts related to social data science and my experience as a PhD student. I’ll also share resources that I’ve found useful in my research and professional development. My posts will be a blend of academic content and personal reflections.
