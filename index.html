About

Hi! I am a PhD student in Social Data Science at the University of Copenhagen. My research interest is in practical, interdisciplinary methods for developing and evaluating predictive systems, and aligning the technical possibilities with societal needs. Currently, I focus on predictive analytics in policy settings in application to education, algorithmic fairness, and the use of mixed methods and algorithm auditing to support participatory algorithm design. I am also interested in AI governance and computational social science, broadly conceived.
