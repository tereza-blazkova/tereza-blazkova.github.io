About

Hi! My name is Tereza and I am a PhD student in Social Data Science at the University of Copenhagen. My research interest is in practical, interdisciplinary methods for developing, evaluating, and improving data-driven systems and aligning the technical possibilities with societal needs. Currently, I focus on predictive analytics in education, algorithmic fairness, and integrating mixed methods with algorithm auditing for developing participatory algorithm design. My broader research interest includes algorithmic decision-making systems, AI and data governance, computational social science, the impact of AI on society, and sustainability.
