About

Hi! I am a PhD student in Social Data Science at the University of Copenhagen. My research interest is developing and evaluating predictive systems with an emphasis on aligning technical capabilities with societal needs. My current work centers on predictive analytics in policy contexts, focusing on applications in education (dropout early warning systems), on algorithmic fairness, and on integrating mixed methods and algorithm auditing to support democratic participatory algorithm design. I am also interested in AI and computational social science, broadly conceived.
