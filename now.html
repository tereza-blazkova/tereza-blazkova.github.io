What am I doing now?*

Last updated on 28 January, while visiting Cornell University's Information Science department.

Copenhagen, Denmark

I started my PhD in Social Data Science at the University of Copenhagen in October 2024, where I am focusing on:

  • Predicting dropout on a nation-scale and examining the temporal aspects of fairness in predictions throughout student enrollment, with a particular focus on fairness for students with disabilities
  • Designing a participatory approach to develop a model for targeted student counseling, aimed at improving well-being and preventing unnecessary dropout, while also evaluating the intervention based on predictions
  • Investigating the algorithmic fairness of models predicting educational outcomes using granular data from Intelligent Tutoring Systems
